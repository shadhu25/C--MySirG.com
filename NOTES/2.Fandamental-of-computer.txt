What is Computer?
	
	Computer is an electonic device that takes inputs, process it and give output.

What is 0 and 1 ?

	There is nothing like 0 and 1 in Computer.	
	There is no physical signicance of 0 and 1.
	Any information can be encoded as a sequence of 0 and 1.
	
What is Hardware ?

	Hardware is a comprehensive term for all of physical parts of a computer, as distinguished from the data it contains or operates on, and the software that provides instructions for the hardware to accomplish tasks.
	Hardware is anything which is tangible.
