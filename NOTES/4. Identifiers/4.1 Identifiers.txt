Constant

	Any information is constant.
	Data = Information = Constant
	
Type of Constants
	
	Primary constant
		Integers Ex:- 2, -3, 45 etc
		Real Ex:- -2.0, 3.44, 333.44 etc
		
	Secondary Constant
		Array, String, Pointer, Union, Structure, Enumerator

Variables

	Variables are the names of memory locations where we store our data.
	Variable name is any combination of alphabet, digitnand underscore.
	
Keywords 

	predefined words are called keywords.(reserved words)
	there are 32 keywords in C language.
